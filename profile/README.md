**Ocxlylab** — the **O**bject **C**ore e**X**perience **L**aborator**Y** — is a research lab building technology that amplifies human potential instead of replacing it. We work at the intersection of **artificial intelligence**, **neurodiversity**, and **education**, shipping tools and products that are accessible, ethical, and genuinely useful.

```yaml
OCXLYLAB:
  status:    ACTIVE
  focus:     AI · Neurodiversity · Education · Publishing
  mission:   Human-Centered Technology
  location:  Kochi · Digital First
  principle: "Technology should amplify human potential, not replace it."
```
